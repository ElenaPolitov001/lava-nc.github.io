run()

Retrieves commands from the runtime service to iterate through the phases of Loihi and calls their corresponding methods of the ProcessModels. The phase is retrieved from runtime service (service_to_process_cmd). After calling the method of a phase of all ProcessModels the runtime service is informed about completion. The loop ends when the STOP command is received.
